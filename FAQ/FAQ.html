  3. What is Brighton University Marketplace?

    4. Brighton University Marketplace is an online platform that connects buyers and sellers, allowing them to trade products in a convenient and secure manner.

  4. How does Brighton University Marketplace work?

    Brighton University Marketplace provides a platform for sellers to list their products/services, and buyers to browse and purchase them. Sellers create listings with product/service descriptions, pricing, and other relevant information, while buyers can search for items, make purchases, and communicate with sellers through our messaging system.

  5. How do I create an account?

    6. To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and follow the instructions. You will need to provide some basic information and agree to our terms and conditions. Once registered, you can start using Brighton University Marketplace as a buyer or seller.

  6. How do I find products on Brighton University Marketplace?

    7. You can use our search bar located on the homepage to search for specific items or browse through different categories.

  7. How do I contact a seller?

    8. You can contact a seller by clicking on the "Contact Seller" button on the product/service listing page. This will open a messaging interface where you can communicate directly with the seller to ask questions or discuss details.

  8. How do I make a purchase?

    9. Once you find a product you wish to purchase, click on the "Buy Now" button next to that particular item.

  9. How do I sell on Brighton University Marketplace?

    10. To sell on Brighton University Marketplace, you need to create a seller account. Once registered, you can create product listings by providing accurate descriptions, pricing, and any other relevant details.
